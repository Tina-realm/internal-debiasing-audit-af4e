# Downloaded Papers

## Core Papers (Specified in Research Topic)

1. **Lipstick on a Pig: Debiasing Methods Cover up Systematic Gender Biases** (gonen_goldberg_2019_lipstick_on_a_pig.pdf)
   - Authors: Hila Gonen, Yoav Goldberg
   - Year: 2019
   - arXiv: 1903.03862
   - Why relevant: Foundational paper showing debiasing methods hide but don't remove bias

2. **Null It Out: Guarding Protected Attributes by Iterative Nullspace Projection** (ravfogel_2020_INLP_nullspace_projection.pdf)
   - Authors: Shauli Ravfogel, Yanai Elazar, Hila Gonen, et al.
   - Year: 2020
   - arXiv: 2004.07667
   - Why relevant: Primary intervention method (INLP) for our audit

3. **LEACE: Perfect Linear Concept Erasure in Closed Form** (belrose_2023_LEACE_concept_erasure.pdf)
   - Authors: Nora Belrose, David Schneider-Joseph, Shauli Ravfogel, et al.
   - Year: 2023
   - arXiv: 2306.03819
   - Why relevant: Second intervention method (LEACE), with stronger guarantees than INLP

4. **Steering Llama 2 via Contrastive Activation Addition** (panickssery_2023_CAA_steering_llama2.pdf)
   - Authors: Nina Panickssery, Nick Gabrieli, Julian Schulz, et al.
   - Year: 2023
   - arXiv: 2312.06681
   - Why relevant: Third intervention method (CAA/steering vectors)

5. **Representation Engineering: A Top-Down Approach to AI Transparency** (zou_2023_representation_engineering.pdf)
   - Authors: Andy Zou, Long Phan, Sarah Chen, et al.
   - Year: 2023
   - arXiv: 2310.01405
   - Why relevant: Framework for reading/controlling representations; provides theoretical grounding

## Intrinsic-Extrinsic Gap Papers

7. **Choose Your Lenses: Flaws in Gender Bias Evaluation** (orgad_2022_choose_your_lenses_gender_bias_eval.pdf)
   - Authors: Hadas Orgad, Yonatan Belinkov
   - Year: 2022
   - arXiv: 2210.11471
   - Why relevant: Position paper arguing intrinsic metrics create "illusion of success"

8. **On the Intrinsic and Extrinsic Fairness Evaluation Metrics** (cao_2022_intrinsic_extrinsic_fairness_metrics.pdf)
   - Authors: Yang Trista Cao, Yada Pruksachatkun, Kai-Wei Chang, et al.
   - Year: 2022
   - arXiv: 2203.13928
   - Why relevant: Extensive 19-model study showing poor intrinsic-extrinsic correlation

9. **Applying Intrinsic Debiasing on Downstream Tasks** (iluz_2024_intrinsic_debiasing_downstream.pdf)
   - Authors: Bar Iluz, Yanai Elazar, Asaf Yehudai, Gabriel Stanovsky
   - Year: 2024
   - arXiv: 2406.00787
   - Why relevant: Tests INLP/LEACE/Hard-Debiasing on MT; shows distortion and limited behavioral impact

10. **The Gaps between Pre-train and Downstream Settings in Bias Evaluation and Debiasing** (kaneko_2024_gaps_pretrain_downstream_bias.pdf)
    - Authors: Masahiro Kaneko, Danushka Bollegala, Timothy Baldwin
    - Year: 2024
    - arXiv: 2401.08511
    - Why relevant: Quantifies representational damage from FT-based debiasing; shows intrinsic-extrinsic gap
